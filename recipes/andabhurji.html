Anda Bhurji

Description

Egg bhurji, also known as Anda Bhurji, is a scrambled eggs dish which is a popular Indian street food and a breakfast, lunch or dinner recipe. The dish is a riotous mix of colors, spice, and flavors laden with butter and character.

Ingredients
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 large onion
  • 1 tablespoon garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 2 chopped green chilli
Steps
  1. Chop onion finely and saute them
  2. Add garlic and ginger and cook till onions are brown
  3. Add green chillies
  4. Add eggs until they are scrambled
  5. Add the needed masala
